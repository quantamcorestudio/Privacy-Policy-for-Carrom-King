Privacy First Philosophy

We do not collect personal identifiers like your name, email, or phone number. Your gaming profile is completely private.

01

Introduction

Summary

Welcome to Carrom King! This policy outlines our strong commitment to player privacy and explains how details are handled in our game.

Welcome to Carrom King, a game meticulously designed and developed by Quantam Core. We are committed to safeguarding the privacy and security of our players. This Privacy Policy is crafted to help you understand how we handle information when you install, play, or interact with our game.

By downloading or playing Carrom King, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you have any concerns or disagree with any part of this policy, please read on to understand what is collected, or contact us directly.

02

Information We Collect

Summary

We do not collect personal info (name, email, etc.). Only basic, anonymous system and performance logs are processed to optimize your gameplay.

Personal Information

We Collect: NONE

We value your privacy above all. We do not collect any personal identifiers such as:

  • Your full name or alias
  • Physical or billing address
  • Phone numbers
  • Personal email addresses

Non-Personal Data

We Collect: TECHNICAL ONLY

Like many games built with the Unity Engine, our game may automatically collect certain non-personal information, such as:

  • Device model, type, and operating system.
  • Unique device identifiers (like Android ID or Advertising ID).
  • Game performance data (including crash logs and session duration).

This technical data is strictly used to identify crashes, fix bugs, balance game mechanics, and ensure Carrom King runs smoothly on your specific mobile device.

03

Third-Party Services

Summary

We partner with Unity and Google to provide essential game infrastructure, analytics, leaderboards, and ad systems. They collect data according to their own policies.

We use third-party industry-standard services to improve your gameplay experience, track bugs, and provide fair advertising. These partners may collect and process data in accordance with their respective privacy policies. We encourage you to review them:

Unity Services

Game Engine • Analytics • Ads

Powering the core physics, multiplayer mechanics, graphics engine, and display ads of Carrom King. Unity processes gameplay events and device hardware information to keep the game optimized.

Read Unity Privacy Policy

Google Play Services

Achievements • Leaderboards • Updates

Used for online leaderboards, tracking achievements, facilitating game updates, cloud saves, and securing matches against tampering. Google processes account identifiers linked to your Google Play Profile.

Read Google Privacy Policy
04

Children’s Privacy

Age Guard

Carrom King is safe for all ages. We never knowingly collect or ask for data from players under 13.

Our game is designed for all ages, offering a clean, family-friendly environment. We do not knowingly collect, store, or solicit personal information from children under the age of 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and you believe that we have accidentally collected personal data from your child under 13, please contact us immediately. We will take swift, decisive measures to locate and delete that information from our records and ensure our partners do the same.

05

Data Security

Encryption & Integrity

We work closely with industry-standard partners to protect technical data against unauthorized access.

The security of your information is extremely important to us. Although no mobile game or method of electronic transmission over the internet can guarantee 100% security, we take strong, reasonable measures to protect any non-personal information collected by our third-party partners.

These practices are aimed at preventing unauthorized access, accidental loss, alteration, or disclosure of data. We review our partners' security compliance regularly to maintain top-tier protection standards.

06

Changes to This Policy

Evolution

We occasionally update our privacy policy. Check this page anytime to see the latest version.

Quantam Core may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to adapt to new legal regulations, technical requirements, or improvements to our game mechanics.

We will notify our players of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the very top of this policy page. We highly recommend reviewing this page periodically to stay informed about how we safeguard our community.
